Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How long does ceramic coating last?
Ceramic coating typically lasts 2-5 years depending on driving conditions and maintenance. We recommend annual top-ups to maintain optimal protection.
What is the average detailing cost?
Pricing varies based on vehicle size and service package. Basic detailing starts at RM 150, while premium packages including ceramic coating range from RM 800-1200. Contact us for a custom quote.
How long does a full detail take?
A standard detail typically takes 4-6 hours. Paint correction and ceramic coating applications may require an additional day. We always discuss timeline with customers upfront.
Do you offer mobile detailing?
Yes! We provide mobile detailing services within a 15-mile radius of our location. Contact us to schedule a mobile appointment.
Is your service eco-friendly?
Absolutely! We use biodegradable cleaning solutions and eco-friendly products. Our mobile unit includes water reclamation systems to minimize environmental impact.
